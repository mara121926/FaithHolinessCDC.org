About Faith Holiness CDC

Faith Holiness Community Development Corporation is a faith-based nonprofit organization dedicated to empowering lives and strengthening communities.

Our Mission

To serve youth, adults, and seniors in our community through free, faith-based programs and services that promote dignity, opportunity, and lasting change.

Our Core Values

  • Faith: Grounded in spiritual principles and values
  • Compassion: Serving those in need with genuine care
  • Partnership: Collaborating with local networks and organizations
  • Empowerment: Building capacity and independence
  • Excellence: Delivering quality services with integrity

What We Do

🏠

Housing Assistance

Support and resources to help individuals and families secure stable, safe housing.

💼

Employment Services

Job training, placement assistance, and career development programs for economic stability.

📚

Education & Tutoring

Academic support and educational programs for youth of all ages.

🧠

Mental Health Support

Counseling and mental wellness resources to support holistic health.

👥

Community Programs

Faith-based community events, support groups, and social services.

❤️

Senior Services

Programs and support specifically designed for seniors in our community.

Our Partnerships

We work collaboratively with local networks and organizations to maximize our impact and reach more people in need.

Why Partnerships Matter

Our community partnerships amplify our mission and allow us to serve more effectively. By working together with local organizations, government agencies, and faith communities, we create a comprehensive support network that addresses the root causes of poverty and provides pathways to sustainable change.

Our Partners Include:

  • Local faith-based organizations
  • Community development agencies
  • Educational institutions
  • Healthcare providers
  • Government services agencies
  • Job training and employment centers
  • Housing authorities
  • Mental health and counseling services

To discuss partnership opportunities, please contact us at info@faithholinesscdc.org
